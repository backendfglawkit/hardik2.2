5-Step Finishing Process

Finishing Processes are as follows:-

  • Grinding Battery boxes.
  • Washing and Double washing system.
  • Drying with latest tech system.
  • Colour wise Sorting.
  • Cutted Granuels live Virgin Quality.

Behind our remarkable journey stands Mr. Kirti Kumar Mangla, a name synonymous with leadership in the lead and battery recycling industry since 2000. His relentless pursuit of sustainable practices and innovation led to a groundbreaking idea - the utilization of battery plastic byproducts to create top-tier PPCP granules.

At Rudraksha Enterprises, we believe in the power of innovation, responsibility, and sustainable practices. Our journey, which began in 2008 in the vibrant city of Jaipur, Rajasthan, has been nothing short of transformative. Founded by industry luminary Kirti Kumar Mangla, we have emerged as pioneers in the manufacturing of PPCP (Polypropylene Co-Polymer) Granules derived from battery-grade plastic.

Setting New Standards We take immense pride in being the first mover in Rajasthan's PPCP manufacturing industry. Our humble beginnings at the Sarna Dungar Industrial Area in Jaipur were marked by a vision to not only produce high-quality granules but also to make a positive impact on the environment.

A Diverse Client Base Over the years,we have had the privilege of serving more than 200 clients and brands across diverse sectors, including household products, paint bucket manufacturing, automobile components, and packaging industries. Our PPCP granules have become the cornerstone of their manufacturing processes, helping them achieve excellence in their respective domains.
