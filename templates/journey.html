Welcome To Our Rudrax!

Our Journey

Rudraksha Enterprises - Pioneers in PPCP Granules Manufacturing

  • 2008

    Our Humble Beginnings

    This journey began in 2008 when our visionary founder, "Kirti Kumar Mangla", decided to transform an industry and make a significant impact on the environment.At "Rudraksha Enterprises", we pride ourselves on being trailblazers in the manufacturing of PPCP (Polypropylene Co-Polymer) Granules derived from battery-grade plastic.

  • Boom in Legacy

    Breaking New Ground in Rajasthan

    Rudraksha Enterprises took its first steps in the Sarna Dungar Industrial Area of Jaipur, Rajasthan. We embarked on a journey that would not only revolutionize the plastic manufacturing industry but also set a benchmark for environmental responsibility.

  • Client Faith

    Diverse Clientele

    Over the years, we have catered to the needs of more than 200 clients and brands spanning various industries. From households to paint bucket manufacturers, automobile companies to packaging industries, our high-quality PPCP granules have become an integral part of their production processes

  • Sister Concern

    Penta Exim Limited

    Rudraksha Enterprises is not the only feather in Mr. Mangla's cap. Our sister concern, Penta Exim Limited, is dedicated to battery recycling with plants in Jaipur and Nepal. This synergy between Rudraksha Enterprises and Penta Exim Limited ensures a holistic approach to responsible and sustainable manufacturing practices

  • In Present

    Today, Rudraksha Enterprises stands tall as a testament to innovation, commitment, and environmental consciousness. Our journey is far from over, as we continue to evolve, adapt, and lead the way in the PPCP granules manufacturing industry. Join us in our pursuit of a greener, more sustainable future.
